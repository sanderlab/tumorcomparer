Choosing Cell Lines for Ovarian Cancer Research

SK-OV-3, A2780, OVCAR-3, IGROV-1, and PA-1 are some of the most widely used cell lines for ovarian cancer research. Often researchers have questions on selecting the most appropriate cell line(s) for particular research uses.

TumorComparer utilizes several data types (e.g., mutation, copy number, and expression) to perform a comparison of The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA) tumor data to experimental cell line models from the Cancer COSMIC Cell Line Project (CCLP) for several cancer types. Please refer to Sinha et al., 2021. DOI: 10.1016/j.crmeth.2021.100039 for details on the methodology.

This page provides a pre-computed analysis for ovarian cancer cell lines. This analysis provides insight into the differences between the ovarian cancer cell lines SK-OV-3 and A2780; SK-OV-3 and OVCAR-3; and SK-OV-3 and IGROV-1.

If you use content from this page, please cite Sinha et al., 2021. DOI: 10.1016/j.crmeth.2021.100039.
